What's happening and why?

Thousands of studies conducted by researchers around the world have documented temperature increases on Earth's surface, as well as atmospheric and oceanic rises. A lot of other aspects of the global environment are also shifting. Extreme high temperatures and heavy rainfall events are rising, glaciers and snow cover are decreasing, and the sea ice is disappearing. Seas are warming, rising and getting warmer

Trends are all consistent and expected to continue in a warming world.

Many lines of evidence show that human activities , particularly heat-trapping greenhouse emissions Gasses from the combustion, deforestation and land use of fossil fuels are primarily responsible for climate change Changes observed in the industrial era , especially during the last six decades.
